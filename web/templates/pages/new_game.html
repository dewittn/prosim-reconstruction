Game Overview

In PROSIM, you'll manage:

  • Production - Schedule machines to produce parts (X', Y', Z') and assemble products (X, Y, Z)
  • Workforce - Train operators to improve efficiency, manage scheduling
  • Inventory - Order raw materials and purchased parts with lead times
  • Quality & Maintenance - Budget for quality control and equipment maintenance

Your goal is to minimize total costs while meeting demand. Shipping occurs every 4 weeks, and unfulfilled demand carries penalties.
